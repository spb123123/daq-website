What is DAQ?

DAQ is a decentralized ERC-20 token focused on minimizing market volatility while providing ease of access for users of all experience levels. It's engineered for reliability, growth, and community-driven governance.

Key Features

Stability

Designed to reduce volatility in the crypto market through algorithmic mechanisms and responsible tokenomics.

Ease of Use

User-friendly interfaces and beginner-focused resources make DAQ a perfect entry point into decentralized finance.

Community Ownership

Holders have governance rights via a DAO, giving everyone a voice in the future of DAQ.
